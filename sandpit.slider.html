Writing

CONTEXT

I founded Dawn Press in late 2010, in doing so becoming Australia’s first dedicated Risograph printer. As an alternative means of production the printer created a platform for experimentation.

CHALLENGE

Developing a niche business in a declining industry.

OUTCOME

An internationally recognised brand that acted as an experimental platform for artists and small businesses alike.

PROCESS, DESIGN

As the print industry declines, consumers are becoming increasingly shut out of the production process with businesses becoming increasingly industrialised in order to stay afloat. Responding to this trend, I built Dawn Press around an opposing model that instead celebrated collaboration with clients.Ideas were explored through continuous dialogue and design iterations resulting in an end product that genuinely reflected each client.Integral to the process was an understanding of the requirements for the printed material; namely who the work was intended for and the final context the printing would exist. It was this foundation of natural inquiry that has led me to pursue a career in user experience design.
