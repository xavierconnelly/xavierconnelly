Xavier Connelly is a designer focused on creating supportive technology to enhance peoples lives.

His practice is underpinned by thorough research to inform the decisions made, ensuring the right problems are being solved for.

Recently he has begun teaching himself front end code in an attempt to better understand the world of engineers.

Before crossing over to the digital world he founded and ran the internationally renowned printing business Dawn Press for close to a decade.
